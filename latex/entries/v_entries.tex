\dictentry{Vicinity Card}{/vɪˈsɪnɪti kɑːrd/, \textit{n.}}{
A type of contactless smart card or RFID tag that can be read from a distance
of up to 1.5 meters (5 feet). Unlike proximity cards that require a "tap,"
vicinity cards are designed for hands-free convenience.

\crossref{\textbf{ISO/IEC 15693}, \textbf{Proximity Card}}
}
