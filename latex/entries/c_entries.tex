\dictentry{CCMS}{/see see em ess/, \textit{abbr.}}{
Credit Card Management System. A software application that helps banks and financial institutions manage the complete lifecycle of credit cards. This includes issuing new cards, setting credit limits, tracking transactions, generating billing statements, processing payments, and handling customer disputes. It's like the behind-the-scenes control center that makes credit cards work from application to cancellation.

\example{When you apply for a new credit card online, the bank's CCMS system processes your application, assigns you a card number, sets your credit limit, and begins tracking all your purchases.}
